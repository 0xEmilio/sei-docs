# Faucets

## What is a Faucet?

Faucets distribute small amounts of tokens to enable developers and users to deploy and interact with contracts on testnets.

## Available Faucets

**For `atlantic-2` (public testnet)**

- [(Discord gated) Sei Faucet](https://atlantic-2.app.sei.io/faucet)

For more faucet options, please visit our [chain registry](https://github.com/sei-protocol/chain-registry/blob/main/chains.json).
